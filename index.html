FAQs For Therapy

What is therapy, and how can it help me?

Therapy involves personalised, one-on-one sessions with a psychologist to support emotional healing, personal growth, and mental well-being. It helps address issues such as anxiety, depression, stress, relationship challenges, and self-esteem concerns by providing strategies tailored to your needs.

How long does a typical therapy session last?

Most sessions last 50-60 minutes. However, the duration can vary depending on individual needs and treatment goals.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, therapy is confidential. The psychologist is bound by ethical and legal guidelines to protect your privacy.

FAQs For EAP

What is an Employee Assistance Program (EAP)?

EAP is a corporate wellness solution designed to enhance employee mental health, productivity, and success. It includes counselling, stress management, conflict resolution, and workshops on work-life balance.

How does EAP benefit employees and organisations?

EAP reduces workplace stress, improves productivity, fosters a supportive work environment, and helps employees address personal and professional challenges effectively.

Can small businesses access EAP services?

Yes, our EAP services are scalable and can be tailored to meet the needs of small, medium, and large businesses.

FAQs For PARENTING SUPPORT

What kind of parenting challenges can we help with?

We provide expert advice on issues such as managing tantrums, improving parent-child communication, supporting children with special needs, and fostering emotional resilience in children.

Do you offer parenting workshops?

Yes, we conduct workshops for parents on topics like positive parenting, stress management, and understanding child psychology.

FAQs For PSYCHOMETRIC ASSESSMENTS

What are psychometric assessments?

Psychometric assessments are in-depth evaluations conducted using standardised tools and evidence-based methods to ensure accurate diagnoses. They help understand mental health concerns and guide effective intervention planning.

What types of assessments do you offer?

We offer assessments for intelligence, personality, behavioural issues, learning disabilities, ADHD, emotional well-being, and career aptitude. Assessments are available on request.

How long does an assessment take?

The duration depends on the type of assessment. Some may take 1-2 hours, while others may require multiple sessions.

Are assessments suitable for children?

Yes, we provide assessments tailored to children, adolescents, and adults, ensuring age-appropriate tools and methods.
